# Sulfuric Acid Storage and Handling - Full Project Documentation
**Project:** San Nicolas Sulphuric Acid Terminal  
**Document No:** 352024-G-DC-001 (Consolidated)  
**Date:** 19-Feb-2010  
**Client:** Naviera Petral S.A.  
**Consultant:** Sandwell Engineering Inc.

---

## 1. PROJECT SCOPE
The project involves the development of a marine terminal for the reception of 40,000 tonnes of 98% sulfuric acid per month.
*   **Vessels:** 10,000 to 20,000 DWT ships.
*   **Unloading Rate:** 500 tph (Acid), 70 m³/h (Water), 10 m³/h (Diesel).
*   **Onshore Facilities:** Booster pump station, 4 x 11,500 MT storage tanks, truck loading stations (4 positions), offices, and support infrastructure.

## 2. GENERAL DESIGN CONDITIONS
### 2.1 Environmental Data
*   **Location:** San Nicolas, Peru.
*   **Ambient Temp:** 12°C Min / 30°C Max.
*   **Design Wind:** 40 knots (Operational), 60 knots (Storm).
*   **Seismic/Tsunami:** Wave height H=4m (18s), Tsunami H=5m with 10m run-up.
